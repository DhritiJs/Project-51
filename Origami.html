Panda Origami

    Facts about Pandas

  • Pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • An adult panda can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day
  • Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day.
  • Panda cubs can climb trees at just the young age of six months!
  • Pandas need at least 2 different bamboo species in their range to avoid starvation

Fox Origami

    Facts about Foxes

  • Foxes can live up to 14 years
  • A fox can run 30 miles per hour
  • Foxes are mostly active at night
  • A group of foxes is called a skulk or leash
  • A male is called a ‘dog fox’ while a female is called a ‘vixen’

Penguin Origami

    Facts about Penguins

  • Penguins are flightless birds.
  • Penguins are primarily found only in the Southern Hemisphere.
  • Depending on the species, a wild penguin can live 15 to 20 years.
  • Penguins spend around half their time in water and the other half on land.
  • A group of penguins in the water is called a raft but on land they’re called a waddle

Teddy Bear Origami

    Facts about Teddy Bears

  • September 9th is National Teddy Bear Day
  • The official term for a teddy bear collector is an ‘arctophile’.
  • The teddy bear was named after President Theodore Roosevelt

Butterfly Origami

    Facts about Butterflies

  • Butterflies taste with their feet
  • Butterflies often live just a few weeks
  • Butterflies need heat to be able to move
  • Butterflies can see colours that humans can’t.
  • Butterflies have four wings, not two as you may think.
